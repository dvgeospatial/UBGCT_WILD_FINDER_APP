Welcome to the Wild Finder App!

Discover the Biodiversity of the Upper Bure & Glaven Conservation Triangle

A growing community of nature lovers have used iNaturalist to log a remarkable diversity of wildlife across the Upper Bure & Glaven Conservation Triangle. Use this Wild Finder App to browse their observations on the map. To get started, use the menu on the right to select a wildlife group that interests you — choose from birds, insects, plants, fungi, mammals and more to see wildlife observations recorded by people using the iNaturalist app. At the initial zoom level the map displays a dark blue grid showing where sightings for your chosen wildife group are concentrated across the area. As you zoom in close using your mouse wheel or the +/- buttons and individual sighting markers will appear; hover over any marker to see the common and scientific name of species recorded there. Click the scientific name to open a species search and find out more.

Plan Your Nature Adventure

Why not go and see for yourself! To plan your nature walk, tick the Public Rights of Way box to see footpaths, bridleways and byways overlaid on the map, or tick the CRoW Access Land box to reveal areas of open countryside where you have the legal right to roam freely. You can open and close the side panel using the menu button if you want a fuller view of the map.

Become a Citizen Scientist

Nature needs you — and you don't have to be an expert to make an important difference! By downloading the free iNaturalist app on your smartphone, you can record the plants and animals you encounter on your walks. Every photo you snap and share helps us understand and protect our local environment. Best of all, your own discoveries will appear on this map for everyone else to see!
